ASLE CELEBRATES 25 YEARS

In October of 1992, the Association for the Study of Literature and Environment was founded by an intrepid and engaged group of scholars and writers at the WLA Conference in Reno, Nevada. We proudly celebrated our 25th Birthday​ in 2017, and are honored that Poets & Writers Magazine has featured ASLE to commemorate this milestone.
